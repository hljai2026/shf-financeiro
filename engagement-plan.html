Engagement Overview

Sweet Home Furniture (Saugus, MA) acquired its current location approximately 2 months ago. The store has active sales, an established vendor network, a franchise agreement with Chris, and a team of 4 sellers — but the financial infrastructure is still at an early, informal stage.


This engagement provides structured financial support to build visibility, reduce risk, and create the operational foundation needed for a healthy, sustainable business. Scope covers financial organization, reporting, and process-building — not tax preparation, legal, or payroll compliance.

Ground Rules for This Engagement
1
One source of truth. Everything goes into the Operating Workbook. The current spreadsheet becomes read-only historical data.
2
No mixing personal and business. Any owner loans are tracked separately and have a documented repayment plan.
3
Monthly close. By the 5th business day of each month, the prior month is closed and signed off.
4
Transparency on risks. Anything flagged gets addressed — not ignored. Especially sales tax and outstanding settlements.
5
Cleyton reviews every deliverable. Nothing is final until he has confirmed the numbers match his reality.
